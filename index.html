OpenStreetMap: Impact in Disaster and Development

September 14-15, 2017 | Ottawa, Canada.

Join us for the third annual Humanitarian OpenStreetMap Team “HOT Summit”

On September 14-15, HOT will convene for a global community meeting. The entire HOT community will come together to showcase projects, debate and share ideas, and learn how mapping projects can have impact on the lives beyond the people mapping.
